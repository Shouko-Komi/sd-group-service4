Understanding the Cooling-off Period & Your Consumer Rights

As a consumer, you have the **right to reconsider** certain purchases or contracts, thanks to the **cooling-off period**. This legal protection allows you to **cancel a contract within a specified time frame** without penalties. At SD Group of Companies, we educate consumers about their rights so they can make informed financial and business decisions.

What is a Cooling-off Period?

The cooling-off period is a **legal timeframe** in which consumers can withdraw from agreements such as:

  • Real estate purchases
  • Car and loan agreements
  • Insurance policies
  • Membership subscriptions

How Long is the Cooling-off Period?

Depending on the contract type and state laws, the cooling-off period **varies**. Some typical timeframes include:

  • Property transactions: 3 to 5 business days
  • Car purchases: 1 to 2 business days
  • Insurance policies: 14 to 30 days
